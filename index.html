Equipment Guide

The Ghost Hunter's Arsenal

Every ghost hunter needs their set tools. Here's what you'll find in the equipment van, and how to use each item to their full potential.


The Essentials

  • Flashlight - Your best friend while investigating, and in the dark. After all, it is dangerous to go alone.
  • EMF Reader - Tells you where the action is. Measures ghost activity, watch for level 5.
  • Thermometer - Used to find the ghost's favourite room. Keep checking for sub-zero temps.
  • Spirit Box - A method of spectral communication. Speak clearly, stay alone, stay in the dark.
  • D.O.T.S. Projector - Watch for silhouettes passing through the beam. That there is your ghost.
  • Video Camera - Another set of eyes, with night vision. It is essential for catching ghost orbs.
  • UV Flashlight - Look for fingerprints on doors and windows, or footprints near distrubed salt.
3

Advanced Tools

  • D.O.T.S. Projector - Watch for silhouettes passing through.
  • Ghost Writing Book - Place it in active areas.
  • Thermometer - Cold spots indicate ghost presence.
  • Sound Sensor - Track noise activity from the van.

Pro Tips

Always bring a flashlight and your starter equipment. Set up video cameras early to monitor multiple rooms. Keep spare equipment in the van for quick replacements.
